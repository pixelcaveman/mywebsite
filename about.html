Ethan Lo (born 1996, Hong Kong) is an archivist, curator, and reseracher specialising in photogprahy, contemporary art, art history and visual culture. His interest revolves around the in-betweenness of middlebrow art and lens-based media, the moblitiy of the medium allows it to be found in museums and galleries but also in flea markets and hidden in storages. His current research focuses on the role of memory in collecting and preserving photographs.
